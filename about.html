About

DookIsBad is a non-profit organization operating for the public good, bravely sharing vital truths in the face of institutional censorship and the relentless evil of Duke basketball. We are not UNC fans pushing a local rivalry agenda; our strictly not-for-profit mission is fueled entirely by a pure, unadulterated disdain for the Blue Devils. We provide this charitable public service to unite a marginalized, silenced community of fans. As a dedicated non-profit entity, our only bottom line is standing together to hold them accountable for the betterment of the sporting world. We hate Duke, and support the troops.
