About

DookIsBad is a non-profit organization operating for the public good, bravely sharing vital truths in the face of institutional censorship and the relentless evil of Duke basketball. Let the record show that we are absolutely not North Carolina fans pushing a local rivalry agenda; our strictly not-for-profit mission is fueled entirely by a pure, unadulterated disdain for the Blue Devils. We provide this charitable public service to unite a marginalized, silenced community of college hoops fans who simply refuse to suffer through another unearned foul call or insufferable floor slap. As a dedicated non-profit entity, our only bottom line is standing together to hold them accountable for the betterment of the sporting world.
